About

I help small and medium businesses automate the manual work that slows teams down. My focus is on building reliable, maintainable systems — not just one-off scripts that break when something changes.

My background combines data analysis and API engineering with modern AI tooling. I self-host everything on Hetzner infrastructure in Germany, which means your data stays in the EU — important for businesses with GDPR obligations.

Every automation I build is documented, monitored, and designed so your team can understand it without needing me on call every week.
